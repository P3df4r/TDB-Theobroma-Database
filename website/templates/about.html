About Theobroma DataBase

The theobroma database stores publicly available biological data from the Theobroma family, derived from the T. cacao and T. grandiflorum species. In addition, it provides tools for comparing species present in the database and data submitted by the user. The theobroma database is maintained by the Laboratory of ioinformatics and high performance computing (LaBioCAD), located at the Federal University of Pará (UFPA). In addition, during its first version, it received support from CAPES-FAPESPA, FAPESPA and UNESP.
